About Me

My name is Pamela Gutierrez and I consider myself a jack of many trades! I've recently transitioned into the field of web development having graduated from the UC Berkeley Coding Bootcamp not long ago. I've worked on an assortment of personal or collaborative projects including one currently in development for a local San Francisco-based baker. I am eager to continue learning and growing my skills in the industry and hope to also delve deeper into UI and UX design. I enjoy the creative and critical processes involved in full-stack web development and am looking for opportunities that will help me foster those skills while also bringing my hard work and dedication to a meaningful project. Prior to my career in tech, I worked in the hospitality and tourism industry which took me across the world holding different management and leadership positions. I've worked in places as diverse as the Guatemalan jungle and the Australian Outback and have found that this quick adaptability has served me well in my transition into web development. I look forward to seeing where this new chapter takes me!
